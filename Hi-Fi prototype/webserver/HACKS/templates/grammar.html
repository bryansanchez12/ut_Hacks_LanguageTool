Here's a tip

Practice writing is usually the best way to practice your mistakes in grammar, however, reading is the best way to learn it.

Practise Grammar

Change the verb into the past simple. It's to practise the past simple:

They

(speak) French to the waitress.

At the age of 23, he

(become) a doctor.

She

(have) a baby in June.

Put the verb into the present simple. Be careful about the spelling.

John

(study) French at school.

I hope John

(pass) the exam.

The plane

(fly) low over the airport.
